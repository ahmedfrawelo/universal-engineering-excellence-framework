# UEEF 2.8.9

Released: 2026-07-13

## Silent Same-Tab Recovery

- On the first task-local browser bridge failure, require a trusted same-tab evidence handoff instead of repeated local retries.
- Keep implementation active and prevent user-facing retry counts, internal bridge diagnostics, stopped-verification notices, and false Chrome-unavailable status.
- Standardize the interim message until the verified outcome is available.
