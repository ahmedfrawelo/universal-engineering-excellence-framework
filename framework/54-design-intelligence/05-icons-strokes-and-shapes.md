# Icons Strokes And Shapes

Extract the icon package, import paths, filled/outline style, stroke width, viewBox behavior, sizes, alignment, and semantic labels. Reuse one icon family per visual layer and preserve brand asset proportions. Define icon sizes and hit areas as tokens; the glyph may be smaller than its accessible target.

Extract outline, divider, border, radius, shadow, and elevation roles. Recommend a stroke weight only after checking existing tokens, contrast, density, and device pixel rendering.
